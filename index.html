Triple Trouble - Build List (July 2019)

Select a Build:

Banner Berserker provides a moderate amount of damage and stat boosts from banners. It is not too difficult to learn, although maximising your damage takes some effort.

Power Dragonhunter provides a large amount of damage and can provide stability for the second phase of the wurm.

Condition Quickness Firebrand provides a moderate amount of damage and provides quickness and fury for the group.

Alacrity Renegade provides some damage and alacrity for the group.

Power Soulbeast provides a moderate amount of damage, in addition to Frost Spirit and Spotter.

Might Deadeye provides some damage, might and fury for your group.

Reaper provides very large amounts of damage in the first phase and is straightforward to use.

Power/Condition Might Tempest provides some damage, might and fury for your group, in addition to minor healing at the Amber Wurm.

Currently, the optimal composition for every 10 players is:

  • 1 Banner Berserker (for Banner of Strength and Discipline)
  • 1 Power Soulbeast (for Sun Spirit)
  • 1 Alacrity Renegade (for alacrity)
  • 1 Might Tempest (either variant for might)
  • 6 Condition Firebrands (covers quickness and fury)

While the first phase of the wurm can technically be killed within one damage phase, there is very little room for error. In addition, the event takes fairly long to reset. Thus, this composition focuses on completing the first phase within two damage phases, while maximising the damage done in the second phase. Condition Firebrands are chosen as they provide quickness and have consistently high damage potential on the second phase. Might Tempest is preferred as its damage output on the second phase is more consistent than Might Deadeye.

A more flexible composition that completes the event quickly is:

  • 1 Banner Berserker (for Banner of Strength and Discipline)
  • 1 Power Soulbeast (for Frost Spirit)
  • 1 Alacrity Renegade (for alacrity)
  • 1 Might Deadeye or Tempest (for might and fury)
  • 2 Quickness Firebrands (for quickness)
  • 4 DPS Players - viable choices include:
  • Power Berserker (allows you to split banners so you can run Head Butt + Outrage for more Berserk uptime)
  • Power Dragonhunter (provides stability for second phase)
  • Power Soulbeast (can provide a second spirit, such as Sun Spirit)
  • Power Daredevil/Deadeye
  • Power Holosmith
  • Power Reaper
  • Power Weaver
  • Condition Weaver

Builds for other DPS classes (specifically for Triple Trouble) will be available soon.

Unfortunately for Chronomancers, the Firebrand-Renegade combination is easier to use and has higher damage. This means that I will not be covering any mesmer builds, and it seems that mesmers will be stuck in "Block Jail" for this update!
